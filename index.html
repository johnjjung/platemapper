Plate Mapper by Pith Studio

Plate Mapper is here to help you lay our your experiments!

Plate mapper is a free utility that simply creates a printer friendly plate map for your experiments.

Plate mapper is an open project and the code is published. None of your information is transmitted or stored on our servers.

click more info

Plate mapper is free, and we plan on keeping it free.

We've originally designed Plate Mapper for ELISAs, ELISPOTs, and culture dishes... but feel free to use it for anything with a grid.
